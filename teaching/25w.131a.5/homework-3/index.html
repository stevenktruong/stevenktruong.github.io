Common Mistakes

One mistake I saw a few times was something like this:

There are two cases: ∣sn∣=sn\abs{s_n} = s_n∣sn​∣=sn​ or ∣sn∣=−sn\abs{s_n} = -s_n∣sn​∣=−sn​. In either case, we have lim⁡n→∞sn=lim⁡n→∞(−sn)=0\lim_{n\to\infty} s_n = \lim_{n\to\infty} \p{-s_n} = 0limn→∞​sn​=limn→∞​(−sn​)=0. Therefore, lim⁡n→∞∣sn∣=0\lim_{n\to\infty} \abs{s_n} = 0limn→∞​∣sn​∣=0.
