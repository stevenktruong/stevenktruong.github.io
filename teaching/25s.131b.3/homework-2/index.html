Exercise 1.5.7

  1. If YYY is a compact subset of XXX, and Z⊆YZ \subseteq YZ⊆Y, then ZZZ is compact if and only if ZZZ is closed.

  2. Let {Uα}α∈I\set{U_\alpha}_{\alpha \in I}{Uα​}α∈I​ be an open cover of Y1∪⋯∪YnY_1 \cup \cdots \cup Y_nY1​∪⋯∪Yn​. Notice that for any 1≤i≤n1 \leq i \leq n1≤i≤n,
