    for all n,k∈Nn, k \in \Nn,k∈N. By Riesz representation, (C(X))∗\p{C\p{X}}^*(C(X))∗ is the space of positive Borel measures on XXX. Hence, weak-* convergence of {μn}n\set{\mu_n}_n{μn​}n​ means

    We claim that μn⊗μn→μ⊗μ\mu_n \otimes \mu_n \to \mu \otimes \muμn​⊗μn​→μ⊗μ weakly-* in (C(K×K))∗\p{C\p{K \times K}}^*(C(K×K))∗ as well. Indeed, by Stone-Weierstrass, the set

    A={∑k=1nckfk(x)gk(x) | n∈N, ck∈R, fk,gk∈C(K)}A = \set{\sum_{k=1}^n c_kf_k\p{x}g_k\p{x} \st n \in \N,\ c_k \in \R,\ f_k, g_k \in C\p{K}}A={k=1∑n​ck​fk​(x)gk​(x)∣∣​n∈N, ck​∈R, fk​,gk​∈C(K)}
