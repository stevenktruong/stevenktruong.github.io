    (e2πiα)b=(e2πia/b)b=e2πia=1,\p{e^{2\pi i\alpha}}^b = \p{e^{2\pi ia/b}}^b = e^{2\pi ia} = 1,(e2πiα)b=(e2πia/b)b=e2πia=1,
